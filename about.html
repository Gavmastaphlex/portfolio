About

I’m a geek, and proud of it. Having grown up pushing the boundaries of computers, it only seemed natural to finally take the plunge in 2012 and enrol in several IT-related courses. This then led to being employed as a Web Developer in May 2013, and promoted to Senior Web Developer in August 2016.

Rather than being intimidated by previously unknown code or languages, I view it as an exciting challenge that motivates me to want to pull it apart, test it in every possible way, and then figure out how I can use it for the benefit of my users, colleagues and organization.

I also love communicating and liaising with others, and subsequently thrive in a team environment.

Aside from my passion of code, my enjoyment also comes from having a good jam on the guitar/drums, discovering the next epic board game, travelling, creating movies with my GoPro and spending time having adventures with my wife.
